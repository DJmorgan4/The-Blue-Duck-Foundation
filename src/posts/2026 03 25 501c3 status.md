We're proud to announce that The Blue Duck Foundation has received its official IRS determination letter confirming 501(c)(3) public charity status, effective February 18, 2026.
This milestone means all contributions to the Foundation are now fully tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. Our EIN is 41-4361489 and we are classified as a publicly supported charity under IRC 170(b)(1)(A)(vi).
We're now actively building our programs, board, and partnerships. Thank you to everyone who has supported us from the beginning.
